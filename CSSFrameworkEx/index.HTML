JERK CHICKEN BOWL


My favourite comfort food

My favourite food

Obviously the best kind of food is comfort food - and for me, the ultimate is a Jerk Chicken Bowl.
It's warming, its colourful, its low effort, and its also pretty flexible - my 'favourite meal' is different almost every time I have it!


    If you're not already convinced, here are some of the many reasons why I love it:
  • Its colourful! Who needs beige plates of potato when you can have fried peppers and a selection of greens brightening up your plate!
  • Its easy to make! The main flavour is pretty much just Jerk BBQ sauce, which you can just buy in a jar if your're low on time or energy and it will still be just as delicious
  • Its really easy to make in bulk! Throwing a dinner party? Your friends will love this dish too!
  • Its flexible! Hearty dinner? Use coconut rice with peas as a base! Looking for a lighter lunch? Switch the rice out for a salad bed or some nachos!
  • Presentation is key! For the full experience, you can use a wide bowl to serve the meal up in neat little rows. A very simple meal suddenly looks very fancy and you can feel like you did something productive today even if all you really did was read or binge TV!
